Frequently Asked Questions

Q: APPLYING FOR A TRANSCRIPT OF RECORDS
(Graduates/Non-Graduates)
WHO MAY AVAIL:
1. Student who are applying for:

-Honorable Dismissal
-Promotion
-Re-Ranking
-Further Studies
-Employment
-Board Examinations
-Visa Application
-Verification/ Assessment

CHECKLIST REQUIREMENTS

-Accomplished Request form (RF)
-Official Receipt payment made
-1.5 x 1.5 picture with name tag/ white background (4 pcs)
-Documentary Stamps maroon (3 pcs)

CLIENT STEPS

-Upon submission of the accomplished Request Form, Clerk in charge will check the graduate's school record and issue an Order of Payment form to the student.
-CIC will school the date of release of the TOP upon submission of the duplicate copy of the official receipt.
-CIC will verify the graduate's record and draft the transcript of records (TOR).
-Evaluation of the TOR
-Printing of the TOR
-Signing of the TOR
-Issuance of the 2nd copy of the TOR
Q: APPLYING FOR HONORABLE DISMISSAL
WHO MAY AVAIL:
1. Student who are transferring to other school

FORMS TO FILL-UP
-Clearance Form
-Request Form For F-137

CHECKLIST REQUIREMENTS

-Request Letter
-Signed Clearance Form
-University ID
-Form 137
-4 pcs. Documentary stamp maroon
-4 pcs. 1.5 x 1.5 picture with name tag and white background

CLIENT STEPS

-Upon submission of the Request Letter, Clerk-in-Charge will check the student’s permanent record, provide the clearance form and request form for F-137, issue an Order of Payment form and inform the student of the other requirements.
-Upon submission of complete requirements, the information/releasing staff will issue a claim stub to students to come back on the scheduled date of release
-Clerk-in-Charge will prepare the transfer credentials for evaluation
-Evaluation and checking of the student’s transfer credential
-Signing of the transfer credential
-Once signed, CIC will issue the transfer credential to the student

Q: APPLYING FOR CROSS-ENROLLMENT OF SUBJECT/S
WHO MAY AVAIL:
1. Student concern

CHECKLIST REQUIREMENTS
-Request letter for Cross-enrollment with the College Dean’s and Registrar’s recommendation for approval of the Vice-President for Academic Affairs

CLIENT STEPS
-Upon submission of the letter, CIC will check student’s total number of units enrolled, curriculum checklist and other university’s course description of subject/s
-Once completed, CIC will prepare the permit for cross-enrollment and issue it to the student
Q: APPLYING FOR LEAVE OF ABSENCE
WHO MAY AVAIL:
1. Student whose reason are the following:
-Financial
-Family matter
-Pregnancy
-Employment

CHECKLIST REQUIREMENTS
1.Request Letter that the student is requesting to file Leave of Absence and address to:

-Vice-President for Academic Affairs
-Registrar
-College Dean
-ICTD

CLIENT STEPS
1.Accomplish the form No.8 signed by the following:

-College Dean
-Vice-President for Academic Affairs
Registrar

-The original form issued shall be signed by the clerk-in-charge upon submission of approved request otherwise considered null and void
-Submit the accomplished form no.8 to update to the student’s records.

Q: APPLYING FOR CERTIFICATION OF GRADES
WHO MAY AVAIL:
1. Undergraduate and graduate students applying for:
-Scholarship
-Promotion
-Re-ranking
-Temporary document for purposes of transferring to the other school

CHECKLIST REQUIREMENTS
-Request Letter from the student
-2 pcs. Documentary Stamp (Maroon)

CLIENT STEPS

-Evaluation of Certification
-Printing of Certification
-Signing of the Certification
-Issuance of the Certification of Grades
